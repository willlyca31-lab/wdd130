Our mission

Our mission at Go Haiti White Water Rafting is to deliver safe, thrilling, and memorable river adventures for all guests. We are committed to teamwork, environmental respect, and creating fun, life-enhancing experiences on every journey.

History

Go Haiti White Water Rafting was founded with a strong passion for adventure and a deep appreciation for Haiti s natural beauty. Over the years, we have proudly guided visitors and locals alike through exciting and unforgettable river experiences.
